# Privacy Policy for MultiGPT

**Effective Date:** December 6, 2025  
**Last Updated:** December 6, 2025

## Introduction

MultiGPT ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the MultiGPT mobile application (the "App").

## Information We DON'T Collect

**MultiGPT is designed with privacy-first principles:**

### Personal Data
• We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your personal information
• We do NOT access your device's contacts, photos, or other personal files
• We do NOT track your location or device identifiers
• We do NOT collect analytics data about your app usage

### Conversation Data
• We do NOT store your chat conversations on our servers
• We do NOT read, analyze, or process your AI conversations
• We do NOT share your messages with third parties
• We do NOT use your conversations for training or improvement

### API Keys
• We do NOT access, store, or transmit your API keys to our servers
• Your API keys are stored locally on your device only
• We do NOT have access to your AI provider accounts

## Information Storage

### Local Storage Only
All data in MultiGPT is stored locally on your device:
• **Chat History**: Stored in local SQLite database
• **API Keys**: Stored in Android's secure DataStore
• **Settings**: Stored locally in app preferences
• **User Preferences**: Stored locally only

### No Cloud Sync
• No data is synchronized to cloud services
• No automatic backups to external servers
• Complete offline functionality for viewing history

## Third-Party Services

### AI Provider APIs
When you use MultiGPT, your messages are sent directly to your chosen AI providers:
• **OpenAI**: Messages sent to OpenAI's API with your API key
• **Anthropic**: Messages sent to Anthropic's API with your API key  
• **Google**: Messages sent to Google AI Studio with your API key
• **Groq**: Messages sent to Groq's API with your API key
• **Ollama**: Messages sent to your local Ollama instance

**Important:** Each AI provider has their own privacy policies and terms of service. Please review:
• OpenAI Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy
• Anthropic Privacy Policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
• Google AI Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
• Groq Privacy Policy: https://groq.com/privacy-policy/

### Firebase (Optional)
If you enable crash reporting and analytics:
• **Crash Reports**: Anonymous crash data to improve app stability
• **Usage Analytics**: General app usage patterns (no conversation content)
• **No Personal Data**: No personally identifiable information collected

## Permissions

### Required Permissions:
• **Internet**: Required to communicate with AI provider APIs
• **Network State**: To check connectivity status

### No Other Permissions:
• We do NOT request camera, microphone, or storage permissions
• We do NOT access your contacts or location
• We do NOT require unnecessary permissions

## Data Security

### Local Security:
• API keys encrypted using Android Keystore
• Local database encrypted
• Secure data transmission to AI providers (HTTPS)
• No data stored in app's external storage

### Your Responsibilities:
• Keep your device secure with lock screen
• Use strong, unique API keys
• Regularly review your AI provider account activity
• Report any suspected security issues

## Your Rights

### Complete Control:
• **View Data**: All your data is accessible within the app
• **Export Data**: Export conversations anytime
• **Delete Data**: Clear all data through app settings
• **Opt-Out**: Uninstall app removes all local data

### No Account Required:
• No registration or sign-up process
• No user accounts or profiles
• Complete anonymous usage

## Children's Privacy

MultiGPT does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not target children specifically.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted in the app and on our website. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
